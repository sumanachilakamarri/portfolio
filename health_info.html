VIP: Health Informatics using FHIR

Working with a Front-end & Back-end Team to turn Social Determinants of Health - Economic Stability, Education, Social/Community Context, Healthcare, and Built Neighborhood - into usable FHIR resources

General Outline of my role:

  • Project: developing program that outputs FHIR documents Developing initial program infrastructure to accept inputs and control program behaviors I am currently working on a project with a team and the CDC to turn Social Determinants of Health into FHIR resources that doctors can use for context when diagnosing a patient. I am working with a User Research team and backend/development team as part of a data team. My current work includes taking the user research and cleaning existing data so that it's readable and ready for the development team to turn it into FHIR resources. I am also working with the User Research team to learn more about the Social Determinants of Health - Economic Stability, Education, Social/Community Context, Healthcare, and Built Neighborhood - and scoring these into quantifiable metrics to better understand their impact on overall health outcomes.
