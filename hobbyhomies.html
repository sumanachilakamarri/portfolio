UI/UX Design: HobbyHomies

Our prototype is designed to help Georgia Tech freshmen establish supportive peer networks and deal with the transition from highschool to college by matching them with like-minded peers who will encourage them to pursue their hobbies. The app facilitates meaningful connections by using shared interests in hobbies and compatible schedules. By doing so, it not only helps freshmen find peers with similar interests but also aids in helping students manage their time effectively.
