Spotify’s 2020-2021 Hits: Analyzing Key Features of Top Songs

Collaborated with Diya Mohan on a Machine Learning project aimed at uncovering underlying patterns in popular Spotify tracks from the years 2020-2021. We applied PCA for dimensionality reduction, K-means clustering to group songs by audio features, and kernelized SVM to classify songs as popular or not from a dataset with top songs and their features from these years. The project explored the relationship between song characteristics—such as danceability, energy, and tempo—and their impact on popularity, providing insights into genre classification and the underlying factors driving hit tracks

Conclusion

This project helped me to gain valuable insights into factors that influence song popularity on Spotify. Applying various machine learning techniques, namely PCA, K-means clustering, and kernelized SVM, we were able to learn more about audio features that correlate with track success. This project also helped me to learn the power that machine learning can have in any industry.
